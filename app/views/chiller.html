Mobile-First Design

As with all of our prototypes, we like to flip the status quo and take a mobile-first design approach. We start our design process with mobile constraints and considerations and then work our way up to desktop. The mobile-first approach organically led us to a design that’s more content-focused, and therefore more user-focused.

Design Survey

With help from the research team at Chiller, we administered a survey to assess the success of ChillerTV.com’s new design.

71% of the respondents thought the new design was suitable as an authority in the horror genre. Respondents associated the new design with adjectives like thrilling, scary, exciting, and fun.
