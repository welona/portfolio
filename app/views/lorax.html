The Film Site

In collaboration with Universal Pictures, we were inspired by our favorite tree-loving characters to create a site promoting the Lorax. This whimsically animated film gave us a lot to work with when we began ideating and brainstorming.

We realized our vision when we eventually designed, developed, QA’d, and deployed the site. At the end, we even brought to life an interactive “Meet the Characters” feature.
