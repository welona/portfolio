Ideation

After collecting requirements from the brands and understanding their pain-points during the discovery session, we worked with the Product and Engineering teams to brainstorm. Everyone participated in design charrettes and individually sketched and presented their ideas to the group. At the end of the session, we voted for the pages and features we liked the most.

Prototype

Using what we learned from our discovery, our researchers, designers, and developers joined forces to create a clickable, dynamic prototype that best suited the needs of the users.

Our prototype was coded with HTML, CSS, and Javascript to mirror the final product.
