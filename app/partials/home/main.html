Identify

For those who may have limited English language abilities and are unfamiliar with U.S. drugstores and their products, it can be very difficult to identify the right over-the-counter medicine for their symptoms.

Our team has designed an app that will help in finding the right medicine and making sure they feel confident that it will treat their symptoms.
